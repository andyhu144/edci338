# PLN Participation Analysis (25%)

**Due:** March 28, 2026

## Overview
Evaluates your use and analysis of social platforms throughout the course.

## Components
- **Weekly Blogs** — consistent posting throughout the term
- **Community Commenting & Feedback** — responding to peers' posts
- **Mattermost dialogues** — active engagement in course discussions
- **Social Media Participation** — BlueSky, Instagram, YouTube engagement

## Requirements
- Review and comment on peers' blog posts weekly
- Select and polish one blog post for instructor review (first half of term)
- Use variety of media (text, audio, video clips) in posts
- Curate information and resources
- Hyperlink to outside articles, sources, and people
- Document progress from general research to specific interests

## Assessment
Evaluated on quality, consistency, and depth of participation across all platforms.
