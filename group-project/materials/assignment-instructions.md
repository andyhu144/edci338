# Final Group Project (40%)

**Due:** March 28, 2026
**Group Size:** 4-5 members
**Format:** 10-15 minutes (video, podcast, animation, written, or alternative media)

## Required Content
Create a walkthrough of PLN themes, platforms, and practices. Must address:

1. Key characteristics (benefits/negatives) of discussed social media platforms
2. Features that make media technologies effective for personal learning
3. How technology enhances digital identity/reputation
4. Social considerations and data tracking implications
5. Privacy, security, and acceptable use policy strengths/weaknesses
6. Examples of individuals leveraging PLNs to become field leaders
7. How those individuals use platforms to build reputation
8. Success metrics for PLNs
9. References and resources supporting opinions

## Group Work Contract (required)
1. Team procedures and expectations
2. Timelines and milestones
3. Protocols and consequences for non-compliance
4. Specific task assignments with timelines and responsibilities

## Deliverables
- Notify instructor of group membership (via Mattermost)
- Group work contract
- Final project (10-15 min media piece)
- Written outline with themes and goals
- Reference list
- Contribution documentation
- Peer review feedback

## Evaluation Criteria
- Content quality
- Creativity
- References to course content
- Teamwork and leadership contributions
