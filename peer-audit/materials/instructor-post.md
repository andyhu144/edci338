## Peer Blog Audit Assignment

**Due:** Sunday, March 29, 2026

This is not a traditional blog post. Instead, you will audit a peer's blog post with the goal of providing constructive, actionable feedback.

### Required Materials

- WATCH: [Week 12 Class Video](./class-video-week12.md)

### Assignment Requirements

Audit a peer's blog post, focusing on:

1. **Assessing content accuracy, clarity, and critical thinking**
   - Does the post accurately represent the course concepts and readings?
   - Is the argument clear and well-structured?
   - Does the post demonstrate critical analysis rather than simple summary?

2. **Providing positive critique and encouragement**
   - What does the post do well?
   - Where does the author show strong understanding?
   - How can the author build on their strengths?

3. **Suggesting collaborative resources and enhancements**
   - What additional resources could strengthen the post?
   - Are there course readings or videos the author could better integrate?
   - What connections to other course topics could be made?

### Guidelines for Constructive Feedback

- Be specific and actionable -- not just "good post" but explain what works and why
- Frame suggestions positively -- "you might consider..." rather than "you failed to..."
- Reference course materials where relevant
- Consider the post's engagement with both outline and instructor post requirements
- Evaluate use of media, hyperlinks, and attribution
- Comment on the post's digital identity and professional tone
