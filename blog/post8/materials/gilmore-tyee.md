## Rachel Gilmore

Rachel Gilmore is a journalist at The Tyee, a non-profit independent news organization based in British Columbia. Her work focuses on federal politics and investigative journalism.

### Background

Gilmore has been a contributing editor at The Tyee covering federal politics for approximately a decade. Her career in journalism has been varied and wide-ranging, including work as an author, documentary maker, and reporter. Her investigative reporting has sparked four commissions of inquiry in Canada.

### Professional Philosophy

As stated in her bio: "My touchstone in journalism has always been getting the truth out -- no matter where it takes me."

### Relevance to EDCI 338

Gilmore serves as an example of a Canadian journalist navigating the evolving media landscape. Her work at The Tyee, a reader-supported independent publication, illustrates alternative media models in the age of AI and corporate media consolidation. The Tyee's model of reader-supported journalism represents a different approach to media sustainability than traditional advertising-driven models.
