## Blog Post #3: Inclusion & Community Engagement

**Due:** Sunday, February 1, 2026

This week focuses on how PLNs can be leveraged for inclusive community engagement. We examine the role of social media in promoting diversity, the impact of disinformation on communities, and how professional communication policies shape online messaging.

### Questions to Address (merged from both sources)

**From Course Outline:**
- How diverse is your PLN?
- Are you learning from a variety of diverse voices and expanding your understanding of others' views?
- What is a learning outcome of your PLN and how are you ensuring exposure to diversity and inclusion?
- How does social media engage communications?
- How does social media challenge communications?
- Is it inclusive?
- Does your PLN amplify the views of others?
- The policies of employer social media communication expectations
- What are the benefits of a diverse and inclusive PLN in social media sharing that understands where you are coming from with messaging that impacts the community?

**From Instructor Posts Page:**
- Address inclusion in a professional setting of your choice
- Reflect on social media's role in establishing inclusive and diverse messaging
- Consider employer communication policies and their impact
- Consider the impact of disinformation on community engagement
- Discuss PLN diversity benefits

### Assignment

Create a blog post on inclusion in a professional setting of your choice. Reflect on how social media can play a role in helping you establish messaging and themes that are inclusive and diverse.
