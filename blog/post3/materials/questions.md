## Questions to Address in Blog Post #3

### From Course Outline

1. How diverse is your PLN?
2. Are you learning from a variety of diverse voices and expanding your understanding of others' views?
3. What is a learning outcome of your PLN and how are you ensuring exposure to diversity and inclusion?
4. How does social media engage communications?
5. How does social media challenge communications?
6. Is it inclusive?
7. Does your PLN amplify the views of others?
8. What are the policies of employer social media communication expectations?
9. What are the benefits of a diverse and inclusive PLN in social media sharing that understands where you are coming from with messaging that impacts the community?

### From Instructor Posts Page

10. Address inclusion in a professional setting of your choice.
11. Reflect on social media's role in establishing inclusive and diverse messaging.
12. Consider employer communication policies and their impact on community engagement.
13. Consider the impact of disinformation on community engagement (link to Obama's Stanford address).
14. Discuss PLN diversity benefits.

### Suggested Reflection Points

- Connect Dr. Shelley Moore's work on inclusive education to your PLN experience
- Consider how Mark Carney's Davos speech about middle powers and cooperation relates to inclusive community building
- Think about how Obama's warnings about disinformation connect to the challenge of maintaining inclusive, honest discourse in social media
- Reflect on Clark & Aufderheide's vision for participatory public media -- how does this apply to your PLN?
