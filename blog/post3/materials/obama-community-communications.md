## Context

Former President Barack Obama delivered an address at Stanford University about the challenges of disinformation in the digital age. The speech addresses how social media platforms have transformed public discourse, the spread of misinformation, and the role of community in countering harmful narratives online. This video is referenced in EDCI 338 as part of the discussion on community engagement and the impact of social media on public communications.
