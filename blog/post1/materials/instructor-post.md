## Blog Post #1: PLN & Digital Identity

**Due:** Sunday, January 18, 2026

This week we explore the concept of digital identity and networked publics. Digital identity encompasses your entire online presence -- everything you post, share, how you engage, and how you treat other people in digital spaces. It is your footprint on the web: searchable, persistent, and always representing you.

### Key Concepts

- **Digital Identity:** Your presence online -- everything you post, share, how you engage, how you treat other people, and how you use digital tools (Eric Stoller)
- **Networked Publics:** Public spaces restructured by networked technologies
- **Networked Privacy:** Privacy in a world where individual data is interconnected with others' data (danah boyd)
- **Personal Learning Networks (PLN):** Networks of people, resources, and tools supporting ongoing professional learning
- **Digital Footprint:** The searchable, persistent trace of your online activity
