## Relevance to Post 9 (Privacy Compliance)

This DAIS report is particularly relevant to Blog Post #9 because it directly addresses:

- **PIPEDA compliance gaps** in educational AI tools
- Canadian federal privacy law (PIPEDA) and its limitations for protecting youth data
- Provincial privacy frameworks (Ontario's Bill 194, BC's FIPPA)
- The concept of Privacy by Design (Cavoukian framework) as applied to AI in education
- Data flows between students, teachers, EdTech platforms, and third parties
- The tension between educational innovation and privacy protection
- Specific case studies of data breaches in Canadian educational institutions (e.g., PowerSchool breach at TDSB)

### Key Privacy Questions for PLNs

- Is personal data being collected without explicit educational purpose?
- Are third-party analytics compliant with Canadian privacy standards?
- Do students have meaningful control over their information?
