## Questions to Address in Blog Post #2

### From Course Outline

1. What digital platforms are you currently using to develop your professional network?
2. What could you consider in expanding your professional learning network?
3. Consider how an employer would respond if they assessed your social media presence via your digital identity.

### Thread Discussion Questions

4. How does data privacy and security limit and/or promote a PLN?
5. In your network, how can you create a digital identity/reputation?

### From Instructor Posts Page

6. How would employers assess applicant social media?
7. What digital platforms develop professional networks?

### Assignment Components

8. Create a Visitor & Resident (V&R) Map using the OCLC framework.
9. Share your V&R Map with your learning group.
10. Respond to at least one other person in your group about their map.

### Suggested Reflection Points

- Where do your digital tools fall on the Visitor-Resident continuum?
- How does your map differ in personal vs institutional/professional contexts?
- Are there platforms where you are a Visitor that you could become more of a Resident?
- How does the UBC Digital Tattoo privacy quiz relate to your own digital practices?
