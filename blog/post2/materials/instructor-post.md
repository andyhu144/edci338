## Blog Post #2: Visitor & Resident Mapping

**Due:** Sunday, January 25, 2026

This week focuses on the Visitor & Resident (V&R) framework developed by David White and Alison Le Cornu, which provides a way to understand how individuals engage with digital tools and platforms. Unlike the outdated "digital native/immigrant" metaphor, V&R recognizes that the same person may be a Visitor in some contexts and a Resident in others.

### Key Concepts

- **Visitor:** Functional, task-oriented use of technology with minimal social trace
- **Resident:** Identity-building, community-oriented online presence with social traces
- **Personal vs Institutional context:** The same platform may be used differently depending on context
- **Digital reputation:** How your online presence represents you professionally
