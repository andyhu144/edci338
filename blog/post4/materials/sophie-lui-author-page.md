## Sophie Lui - Global News Anchor

**Role:** Anchor of *Global News at 5* and Co-Anchor of *Global News Hour at 6* (Global BC, Vancouver)

### Background

Born and raised in the Lower Mainland of British Columbia. Graduated from BCIT's Broadcast Radio program. Worked at several news radio stations before transitioning to television at CHEK-TV in Victoria, then moving to Global BC in Vancouver. Previously hosted *Global News Morning* and *Global Weekend Mornings*.

### Notable Achievements

- **Leo Award** for Best Host for her travel program *Get Lost*
- **2023 Canadian Screen Award** (with colleagues) for Best Local Newscast for *Global BC News Hour at 6*
- **2023 Shelley Fralic Award** from The Jack Webster Foundation
- Significant investigative reporting for *16x9* and *The New Reality*, with particular recognition for pieces on medically assisted death and green burials
- Regular host of election night coverage in British Columbia

### Community Involvement

- Board member, BGC South Coast BC
- Cabinet member, BC Cancer Foundation's Beyond Belief Campaign

### Relevance to EDCI 338

Sophie Lui serves as a case study in how public media figures navigate social media, maintain professional digital identity, and balance the demands of traditional journalism with the participatory nature of digital platforms. Her career illustrates the intersection of media literacy, public discourse, and professional PLN management.
