## Questions to Address in Blog Post #4

### From Course Outline

1. How do notable individuals use social media?
2. What are the benefits to being in the public eye and having a PLN?
3. Building community with online tools provided by the employer can be limiting -- what are the perceived restrictions and benefits?
4. Delivering information in a connected society requires verifiable resources -- how to build a PLN to rely on?
5. How do veteran storytellers minimize risk in sharing misinformation?
6. What is media literacy? Why is it important? Why is it dismissed?
7. Why should you aim for varied views but factual consensus in your PLN?
8. Identify the risks and benefits of engaging with a public audience in a media space.
9. Consider what the risks for a public figure or person in a position of trust (educator, lawyer, government official) may be?
10. How to best address negative replies and critiques reflective of your personal values and employer social media policy?
11. Open dialogues about media literacy and factual information can create conflict -- why does this happen?
12. What is the benefit of having a PLN that values media literacy?
